Banned Books over The Years

This chart represents a timeline of some notable books: when they were banned, when the ban was lifted, and where. Looking at when and where a specific book was banned gives us insight into the world. For example, the works of Einstein were banned in Germany by the Nazis in 1925 to promote Anti-Semitism, and branded his work as "Jewish Physics".
These custom visualizations were created using D3.js. They show a more traditional Gantt chart and a more innovative one representing a stack of books.
